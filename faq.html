Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Last updated: June 23, 2025

General Questions

Q1: What is Sair Karo?

A: Sair Karo is your one-stop online platform for booking bus, train, and flight tickets easily and securely. We aim to provide a seamless travel planning experience.

Q2: How do I create an account?

A: You can create an account by clicking on the "Signup" button in the top right corner of the homepage and following the registration process.

Q3: Is my personal information safe with Sair Karo?

A: Yes, we take your privacy seriously. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for detailed information on how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Q4: How can I contact customer support?

A: You can reach our customer support team via the "Contact Us" page, or by using the live chat widget available on most pages for instant assistance from our AI travel assistant.

Booking Questions

Q5: How do I book a bus/train/flight ticket?

A: On the homepage, use the search widget. Select your travel mode (Bus, Train, or Flight), enter your origin, destination, and date(s) of journey. Click "Search" to find available options and proceed with your booking.

Q6: Can I book a round trip?

A: Yes, for bus and flight bookings, you can select the "Return Date" option in the search widget to book a round trip.

Q7: How will I receive my ticket?

A: Once your booking is confirmed, your ticket details will be sent to your registered email address and will also be accessible in your "My Bookings" section if you are logged in.

Q8: What if my payment fails?

A: If your payment fails, please check your internet connection and try again. If the issue persists, contact your bank or try an alternative payment method. If your account is debited but the booking is not confirmed, the amount will typically be refunded automatically within a few business days.

Cancellation and Refunds

Q9: How can I cancel my booking?

A: Cancellation procedures vary based on the service provider and ticket type. Please visit your "My Bookings" section to manage your existing bookings or refer to our Refund Policy for detailed steps and conditions.

Q10: What is your refund policy?

A: Our refund policy outlines the terms and conditions for cancellations and refunds. You can find the full details on our Refund Policy page.

Other Questions

Q11: How do I report an issue or provide feedback?

A: You can report an issue through the "Report an Issue" link in the footer or submit general feedback via our "Feedback" page.

Q12: Where can I find information about offers and deals?

A: All our exclusive offers and deals are displayed on the homepage under the "Exclusive Offers & Deals" section. Keep an eye on this section for the latest discounts!
