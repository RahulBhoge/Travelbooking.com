Customer Service Representative

Location: Hyderabad, India

About the Role

TravelBooking.com is seeking a dedicated and empathetic Customer Service Representative to assist our users with their travel inquiries and booking needs. You will be the first point of contact for many of our customers, providing exceptional support and ensuring a positive experience.

Key Responsibilities

  • Respond to customer inquiries via phone, email, and chat in a timely and professional manner.
  • Assist customers with booking, modifications, cancellations, and general travel questions.
  • Provide accurate information regarding travel policies, procedures, and service offerings.
  • Resolve customer complaints and issues effectively and efficiently.
  • Document customer interactions and solutions in our CRM system.
  • Collaborate with other departments to resolve complex customer issues.
  • Maintain a high level of customer satisfaction.

Required Skills & Experience

  • 1+ year of experience in a customer service role, preferably in travel or hospitality.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
  • Strong problem-solving abilities and a customer-first mindset.
  • Ability to multitask and work effectively in a fast-paced environment.
  • Proficiency in using CRM software and common office applications.
  • Empathy, patience, and a positive attitude.
  • Ability to work flexible hours, including weekends and holidays, as needed.

Bonus Points (Nice to Have)

  • Fluency in multiple languages.
  • Experience with travel booking systems.
  • Familiarity with global travel destinations and regulations.

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary and benefits.
  • Comprehensive training program.
  • Opportunity to be the face of our company and make a direct impact on customer satisfaction.
  • A supportive team environment focused on growth.
  • Travel perks and discounts.
