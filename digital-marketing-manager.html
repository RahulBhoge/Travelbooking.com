Digital Marketing Manager

Location: Delhi, India

About the Role

TravelBooking.com is looking for a dynamic Digital Marketing Manager to lead our online marketing efforts. You will be responsible for developing, implementing, and managing marketing campaigns that promote our company and its products/services. Your work will play a crucial role in driving traffic, acquiring customers, and increasing brand awareness across various digital channels.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and execute comprehensive digital marketing strategies (SEO, SEM, social media, email, content marketing).
  • Manage and optimize paid advertising campaigns (Google Ads, social media ads).
  • Conduct keyword research, competitive analysis, and market trends to identify new opportunities.
  • Analyze website traffic and campaign performance using tools like Google Analytics.
  • Collaborate with the content team to create engaging and relevant marketing materials.
  • Oversee social media presence and engagement strategies.
  • Stay up-to-date with the latest digital marketing trends and technologies.

Required Skills & Experience

  • 5+ years of experience in digital marketing, with a focus on SEO, SEM, and social media.
  • Proven track record of successfully planning and executing digital marketing campaigns.
  • Strong understanding of digital marketing concepts, strategies, and tools.
  • Proficiency with Google Analytics, Google Ads, and social media advertising platforms.
  • Excellent analytical and reporting skills.
  • Strong communication, interpersonal, and leadership skills.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.

Bonus Points (Nice to Have)

  • Experience in the travel or e-commerce industry.
  • Knowledge of CRM software.
  • Familiarity with A/B testing and conversion rate optimization (CRO).
  • Experience with marketing automation platforms.

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary and benefits package.
  • Opportunity to shape the brand's online presence.
  • A dynamic and creative work environment.
  • Professional development and growth opportunities.
  • Impactful work in a rapidly evolving industry.
