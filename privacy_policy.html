Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Sair Karo! Your privacy is incredibly important to us. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, process, and disclose your information when you use our website or services to plan and book your travel experiences. By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

We are committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring a safe and enjoyable journey, both online and offline.

Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our travel booking services to you.

1. Personal Data:

This includes information that can be used to identify you directly or indirectly, crucial for booking and managing your trips:

  • Contact & Identity Information: Name, email address, phone number, physical address, date of birth, gender.
  • Travel Document Details: Passport number, visa information, frequent flyer numbers (only if you provide them).
  • Booking & Reservation Data: Travel dates, destinations, flight and hotel preferences, special requests (e.g., dietary needs, accessibility).
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details (processed securely by third-party payment gateways; we do not store full card numbers), billing address.
  • Account Information: Username, password (hashed), profile preferences, booking history.

2. Usage Data:

Information automatically collected about your access and use of our services to understand trends and improve user experience:

  • IP address, browser type and version, operating system.
  • Pages visited on our site, time spent on those pages, referral sources.
  • Device identifiers (e.g., mobile device IDs), language preferences.
  • Search queries for destinations, flight routes, or hotels.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies:

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your Browse and booking experience, track activity, and gather information. This helps us remember your preferences and show you relevant travel offers.

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Essential for website functionality (e.g., remembering items in your travel cart).
  • Performance Cookies: Collect anonymous data on how users interact with the website to improve its performance (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your choices (e.g., preferred currency, last searched destination).
  • Targeting/Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver travel-related advertisements that are more relevant to you and your interests.

You can configure your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, some parts of our Service related to personalized recommendations or streamlined booking might not function optimally.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes to facilitate your travel bookings and improve your overall experience:

  • To process and confirm your flight, hotel, and other travel bookings.
  • To manage your account and provide customer support related to your travel plans.
  • To personalize your travel experience, offering tailored recommendations for destinations, flights, or hotels based on your past searches and bookings.
  • To send you important travel updates, booking confirmations, itineraries, and alerts (e.g., flight changes).
  • To improve, optimize, and expand our website and services based on user behavior and feedback.
  • To send you promotional offers, newsletters, and information about new destinations or travel deals that may interest you (you can opt-out at any time).
  • To detect, prevent, and address fraud or other illicit activities.
  • To comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.

Sharing Your Information

To provide our comprehensive travel services, we may need to share your personal information with specific third parties:

  • Travel Service Providers: We share necessary information with airlines, hotels, car rental companies, tour operators, and other travel-related service providers to fulfill your bookings. This includes your name, passport details (if required), travel dates, and special requests.
  • Payment Processors: Your payment information is shared with secure third-party payment gateways to process transactions.
  • Analytics & Marketing Partners: We may share aggregated or anonymized data with analytics providers (like Google Analytics) to understand website usage and with marketing partners to deliver relevant advertisements.
  • Law Enforcement & Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law, subpoena, or if we believe it's necessary to protect our rights, property, or safety, or the safety of our users or the public.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your personal information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.
  • With Your Consent: We may share your information for any other purpose with your explicit consent.

We only share the minimum necessary information required to perform specific functions and always strive to ensure these third parties adhere to strict data protection standards.

Data Security

The security of your personal data is a top priority for us. We implement robust technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, alteration, and disclosure. These measures include data encryption, firewalls, and secure server environments.

However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. We encourage you to use strong, unique passwords for your account and to be cautious about sharing personal information online.

Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., GDPR, CCPA), you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The Right to Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • The Right to Rectification: You can request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • The Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): You can request that we delete your personal data under certain conditions.
  • The Right to Restrict Processing: You can request that we limit the way we use your personal data under certain conditions.
  • The Right to Object to Processing: You can object to our processing of your personal data under certain conditions.
  • The Right to Data Portability: You can request that we transfer your collected data to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.
  • The Right to Withdraw Consent: If we rely on your consent to process your data, you have the right to withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@saikaro.com. We will respond to your request in accordance with applicable laws.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to other websites that are not operated by us (e.g., airline sites, hotel portals, tourist attractions). If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit, as their practices may differ from ours. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13 ("Children"). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and become aware that your child has provided us with Personal Data, please contact us. If we discover that we have collected Personal Data from children without verifiable parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We may also notify you via email or a prominent notice on our website prior to the change becoming effective. We advise you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
