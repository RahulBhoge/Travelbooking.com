Backend Engineer (Node.js)

Location: Bengaluru, India

About the Role

We are looking for a talented Backend Engineer with strong Node.js experience to build and maintain the server-side logic of our applications. You will be responsible for developing high-performance APIs, ensuring data integrity, and collaborating with frontend developers to deliver robust and scalable solutions for our travel platform.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design, develop, and maintain efficient, scalable, and secure Node.js-based APIs and services.
  • Work with databases (e.g., MongoDB, PostgreSQL) to manage and store application data.
  • Integrate with third-party services and APIs (e.g., payment gateways, external booking systems).
  • Write unit and integration tests to ensure code quality and reliability.
  • Collaborate with frontend developers to define API contracts and ensure smooth integration.
  • Participate in architectural discussions and contribute to system design.
  • Troubleshoot and debug production issues.

Required Skills & Experience

  • 3+ years of experience in backend development with Node.js.
  • Strong proficiency in JavaScript/TypeScript.
  • Experience with Node.js frameworks like Express.js, Koa, or NestJS.
  • Solid understanding of database systems (SQL and NoSQL) and ORMs/ODMs.
  • Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure) and microservices architecture.
  • Experience with version control systems (e.g., Git).
  • Knowledge of security best practices for API development.
  • Strong problem-solving and analytical skills.

Bonus Points (Nice to Have)

  • Experience with Docker and Kubernetes.
  • Familiarity with message queues (e.g., Kafka, RabbitMQ).
  • Understanding of caching mechanisms (e.g., Redis).
  • Experience in the travel or e-commerce industry.

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits.
  • Opportunity to work in a fast-paced, innovative environment.
  • Continuous learning and professional growth opportunities.
  • A collaborative and supportive team culture.
  • Impactful work on a global travel platform.
