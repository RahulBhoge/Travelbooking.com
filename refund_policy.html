Refund Policy for Sair Karo

Last updated: June 23, 2025

Introduction

This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which Sair Karo processes refunds for bookings made through our platform. As an intermediary, Sair Karo's refund process is largely governed by the terms and conditions of our travel service providers (airlines, bus operators, hotels, etc.).

By making a booking through Sair Karo, you agree to this Refund Policy and the specific cancellation and refund policies of the respective service provider.

Cancellation & Eligibility

Refund eligibility is primarily determined by the cancellation policy of the specific airline, bus operator, railway, or hotel you booked. Key points include:

  • Timing: Most service providers have strict deadlines for cancellations. Cancellations made after this deadline may not be eligible for a refund or may incur higher penalties.
  • Ticket Type: Certain ticket fares (e.g., promotional, non-refundable) may have little to no refund value, regardless of when cancelled.
  • Supplier Fees: Service providers may levy cancellation fees. These fees will be deducted from any eligible refund amount.
  • Sair Karo Service Fees: Any service fees charged by Sair Karo for facilitating the original booking are generally non-refundable.
  • How to Cancel: All cancellations must be initiated through the Sair Karo platform or by contacting our customer support directly. We cannot guarantee refunds for cancellations made directly with the service provider without prior notification to us.

Refund Process

Once you initiate a cancellation through Sair Karo:

  1. Request Submission: Your cancellation request is submitted to the respective service provider.
  2. Provider Confirmation: The service provider reviews the request based on their policy and confirms the eligible refund amount (if any).
  3. Sair Karo Processing: Upon receiving confirmation from the service provider, Sair Karo will process the refund to your original payment method.
  4. Notification: You will be notified via email about the status of your refund, including the amount, once processed.

Please note that the refund amount will be subject to any applicable cancellation fees from the service provider and Sair Karo's non-refundable service fees.

Refund Timelines

The time it takes for a refund to be credited back to your account can vary significantly. Factors influencing this include:

  • Service Provider Processing: Airlines and other providers may take several business days or even weeks to process a cancellation and release funds.
  • Bank Processing: Once Sair Karo processes the refund, your bank or payment processor may take an additional 3-10 business days to post the credit to your statement.

Typically, refunds can take anywhere from **7 to 21 business days** to reflect in your account from the date of cancellation confirmation. In some cases, especially for international transactions or specific airlines, it might take longer. We appreciate your patience during this process.

Non-Refundable Items

Please be aware that the following items are generally non-refundable:

  • Sair Karo's own service fees charged at the time of booking.
  • Any third-party convenience fees (e.g., payment gateway charges).
  • Specific fare types or promotions explicitly stated as non-refundable by the service provider.
  • Travel insurance premiums once purchased.
  • Services that have already been utilized (e.g., partial travel completed).

Supplier Policies

It is crucial to understand that each airline, bus company, railway, or hotel has its own specific cancellation and refund policies. We strongly advise you to read these policies carefully before making any booking. These policies are usually accessible during the booking process on our site or on the respective service provider's official website.

Any disputes regarding the refund amount or eligibility ultimately lie with the terms set forth by the specific travel service provider.

Force Majeure

In cases of unforeseen circumstances beyond our control (e.g., natural disasters, acts of war, pandemics, government mandates, extreme weather events) that lead to cancellation or disruption of travel services, Sair Karo will adhere to the refund and compensation policies set forth by the respective travel service providers and applicable laws.

While we will do our best to assist you in such situations, refunds for "force majeure" events are entirely at the discretion of the service provider and relevant government regulations.

Changes to This Refund Policy

Sair Karo reserves the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated policy on this page, along with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
