Senior Frontend Developer

Location: Remote / Bengaluru, India

About the Role

TravelBooking.com is seeking a highly skilled and experienced Senior Frontend Developer to join our growing engineering team. You will be responsible for leading the development of our user-facing web applications, ensuring a seamless and intuitive experience for millions of travelers. This role involves working with modern JavaScript frameworks, collaborating closely with UI/UX designers, and contributing to architectural decisions.

Key Responsibilities

  • Lead the design, development, and maintenance of scalable and high-performance frontend applications using React.js.
  • Collaborate with product managers, backend engineers, and designers to translate requirements into technical specifications and elegant user interfaces.
  • Write clean, well-documented, and efficient code, ensuring high quality and adherence to best practices.
  • Optimize applications for maximum speed and scalability.
  • Mentor junior developers and contribute to a culture of continuous learning and improvement.
  • Participate in code reviews, architectural discussions, and sprint planning.

Required Skills & Experience

  • 5+ years of experience in frontend web development.
  • Strong proficiency in JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS3.
  • Extensive experience with modern JavaScript frameworks, particularly React.js and its ecosystem (Redux, Hooks, etc.).
  • Experience with responsive design principles and cross-browser compatibility.
  • Familiarity with RESTful APIs and asynchronous request handling.
  • Proficiency with version control systems (e.g., Git).
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • Strong communication and teamwork abilities.

Bonus Points (Nice to Have)

  • Experience with TypeScript.
  • Knowledge of build tools like Webpack, Vite.
  • Familiarity with UI testing frameworks (e.g., Jest, React Testing Library).
  • Experience in the travel or e-commerce industry.

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary and benefits package.
  • Opportunity to work remotely or from our Bengaluru office.
  • A vibrant and collaborative work environment.
  • Challenging projects with a global impact.
  • Professional development opportunities and a learning budget.
  • Generous travel discounts and perks!
