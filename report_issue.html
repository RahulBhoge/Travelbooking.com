Report an Issue to Sair Karo

Last updated: June 23, 2025

Introduction

At Sair Karo, we are committed to providing you with a seamless and reliable travel booking experience. If you encounter any problems, technical issues, or have concerns regarding our services, we encourage you to report them to us promptly. Your feedback is invaluable in helping us improve and ensure a better experience for all users.

This policy outlines the steps to report an issue, the information you should provide, and what you can expect from our support team.

How to Report an Issue

You can report an issue through the following channels:

  1. Through our Contact Form: The easiest way to report a non-urgent issue is by filling out the dedicated form on our Contact Us page.
  2. Via Email: For detailed reports, you can email our support team directly at support@sairkaro.com.
  3. Live Chat (if available): For immediate assistance with urgent issues during business hours, you may use our live chat feature on the website.
  4. Phone Support: For critical issues requiring immediate verbal communication, you can call us at +91 98765 43210.

Please choose the most appropriate method based on the urgency and complexity of your issue.

What Information to Include

To help us resolve your issue as quickly and efficiently as possible, please include the following information in your report:

  • Your Contact Information: Full name, email address, and phone number associated with your Sair Karo account.
  • Booking ID (if applicable): For issues related to specific bookings (flights, trains, buses).
  • Type of Issue: Briefly describe whether it's a technical bug, payment problem, booking error, content inaccuracy, etc.
  • Detailed Description: Explain the problem clearly and concisely. Include:
    • What you were trying to do.
    • What happened instead.
    • The exact error message (if any).
  • Date and Time of Occurrence: Approximately when the issue occurred.
  • Steps to Reproduce: If possible, list the steps we can take to see the issue ourselves.
  • Device & Browser Information: (e.g., "Chrome on Windows 10," "Safari on iPhone," "Android app version X.X").
  • Screenshots or Videos: Attach any relevant screenshots or short videos that demonstrate the issue. This is highly recommended for visual bugs.

The more details you provide, the faster our team can investigate and resolve the problem.

Our Resolution Process

Once we receive your issue report, here's what you can expect:

  1. Acknowledgement: You will receive an automated email confirmation that your report has been received, usually within minutes.
  2. Initial Review & Prioritization: Our support team will review your report, categorize it, and prioritize it based on its impact and severity.
  3. Investigation: Our technical team will investigate the issue. This may involve replicating the problem and analyzing logs.
  4. Resolution or Workaround: We will aim to resolve the issue directly or provide a temporary workaround. For complex bugs, this might involve a patch or update.
  5. Communication: We will keep you updated on the progress of your issue via email or your preferred communication method.
  6. Confirmation: Once the issue is resolved, we will inform you and may ask you to confirm the fix.

Our goal is to address all issues in a timely manner. Response times may vary depending on the volume of reports and the complexity of the issue.

Types of Issues to Report

Please report any of the following types of issues you encounter:

  • Technical Bugs: Website not loading, features not working, broken links, display errors.
  • Booking Errors: Incorrect ticket details, payment failures, issues with receiving confirmation.
  • Performance Problems: Slow loading times, unresponsiveness.
  • Content Inaccuracies: Outdated information, incorrect prices, missing details.
  • Security Concerns: Suspicious activity, potential vulnerabilities.
  • Accessibility Issues: Difficulties using the site with assistive technologies.
  • General Service Feedback: Anything that hinders your user experience.

Follow-Up & Communication

After submitting your report, please refrain from submitting multiple reports for the same issue, as this can slow down our response time. If you need to provide additional information or follow up on an existing report, please reply to the original email or use the reference number provided in the acknowledgement.

We appreciate your patience and cooperation as we work to resolve your reported issues.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this "Report an Issue" policy from time to time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated policy on this page, along with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
