DevOps Specialist

Location: Remote / Mumbai, India

About the Role

We are seeking a skilled DevOps Specialist to join our engineering team. In this role, you will be crucial in automating, deploying, and managing our infrastructure and applications. You will work closely with development and operations teams to streamline our CI/CD pipelines, ensure system reliability, and implement best practices for cloud infrastructure.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design, implement, and maintain CI/CD pipelines using tools like Jenkins, GitLab CI, or GitHub Actions.
  • Manage and provision cloud infrastructure (e.g., AWS, GCP, Azure) using Infrastructure as Code (Terraform, CloudFormation).
  • Implement and maintain monitoring, logging, and alerting solutions for our applications and infrastructure.
  • Automate deployment, scaling, and management of containerized applications (Docker, Kubernetes).
  • Ensure high availability, performance, and security of our production systems.
  • Collaborate with development teams to optimize application delivery and operational efficiency.
  • Manage version control systems (Git) and branching strategies.

Required Skills & Experience

  • 4+ years of experience in DevOps or SRE roles.
  • Strong experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure).
  • Proficiency in scripting languages (e.g., Python, Bash).
  • Hands-on experience with Docker and Kubernetes.
  • Experience with CI/CD tools (Jenkins, GitLab CI, GitHub Actions).
  • Familiarity with monitoring tools (e.g., Prometheus, Grafana, ELK Stack).
  • Understanding of networking, security, and database concepts in a cloud environment.
  • Excellent problem-solving and communication skills.

Bonus Points (Nice to Have)

  • Experience with configuration management tools (Ansible, Chef, Puppet).
  • Certifications in cloud platforms (e.g., AWS Certified DevOps Engineer).
  • Experience with serverless technologies.
  • Knowledge of GitOps principles.

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary and benefits.
  • Remote work flexibility.
  • Opportunity to work on cutting-edge cloud technologies.
  • A dynamic and supportive team environment.
  • Impactful role in ensuring the reliability of our global platform.
